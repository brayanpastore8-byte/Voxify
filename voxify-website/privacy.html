Privacy Policy

Your privacy is fundamental to us. This notice transparently explains what data is processed by Voxify Music.

1. Data Minimization

Voxify collects only the technical data necessary for operation, such as Server ID, Voice Channel ID, and User ID of the person executing commands to manage the music queue.

2. Voice Privacy

We formally declare that Voxify Music does NOT record, NOT listen to, and NOT store the audio of conversations in voice channels. The bot's architecture is designed exclusively for sending outgoing audio streams.

3. Temporary Storage

Information about the music queue (current playlist) is stored in volatile memory (RAM) and is automatically deleted whenever the bot leaves the voice channel or the session ends.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell or share data with advertising agencies or third parties. The only external interactions occur with official music APIs to retrieve song titles.

5. User Rights

Administrators can request the deletion of any server configuration data by contacting support or simply removing the bot from the server.
